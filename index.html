Independent coursework project · 2026

Diffusion, Flow Matching, and CRH-CFG

I independently completed the publicly available coursework and assignments from CMU 10-799, Diffusion and Flow Matching.

Poster (PDF) Paper (PDF) Code / repository
Implemented
DDPM, straight-path Flow Matching, DDIM, Euler and Heun samplers, and conditional classifier-free guidance.
Experiment setting
CelebA 64×64; Modal L40S; independent ResNet-18 evaluator.
Conditional adherence
Joint-target accuracy increased from 3.1% at w = 0 to 94.5% at w = 2. This result indicates stronger adherence, not general image quality.
Sampler comparison
At matched NFE, Heun showed no Pareto improvement.
CRH-CFG probe
Across 1,000 paired seeds per target, the descriptive result was 98.40% → 98.80% (+0.4 pp). It does not demonstrate improved preservation or conditional quality.
